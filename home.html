Company Background

Sigma Motors is a car dealership company that has been in the business for over 10 years. We have a wide range of cars, from brand new to used, that cater to different budgets and preferences. Our goal is to provide the best car buying experience to our customers.

Target Market

Our target market is anyone who is in the market for a car. We have options for those who are looking for an affordable car for their daily commute, as well as those who are looking for a luxury car for their special occasions.

SWOT Analysis

  • Strengths: Wide range of car options, experienced staff, excellent customer service.

  • Weaknesses: Limited advertising budget, small online presence.

  • Opportunities: Expand online presence, increase marketing efforts, offer more financing options.

  • Threats: Competition from other car dealerships, changes in the economy that affect car sales.

Competitors

Our main competitors in the local area are Auto-Land Motors and Koloi-Land Dealership. However, we differentiate ourselves by offering a wider range of car options and excellent customer service. We also have a team of experienced staff who are knowledgeable about cars and can help our customers make the best buying decisions.
