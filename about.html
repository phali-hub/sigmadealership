About Us

History

Sigma Delership was founded in 2014 by Sethothi Phali, a seasoned automobile enthusiast with a vision to provide high-quality vehicles to customers in the local area. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most reputable car dealerships in the region, with a focus on customer satisfaction and long-term relationships.

Aims and Objectives

At Sigma Dealership, our aim is to provide the best possible service to our customers, from the moment they walk in the door to the moment they drive off the lot in their new vehicle. We are committed to providing a wide selection of quality cars at competitive prices, as well as financing options and a range of after-sales services. Our ultimate objective is to establish Sigma Dealership as the go-to destination for all your automotive needs.
